Ekkamai Vibe is a contemporary Thai–Latin sans-serif typeface family in five weights, designed and released by Ekkamai Foundry. It is built for a generation that wants to speak with clarity, edge, and honesty — whether on a billboard above the expressway, a phone screen at midnight, or the cover of a small-press book down a Bangkok side street.

The family ships in Thin, Light, Regular, Bold, and Heavy weights, with full Thai character coverage (including tone marks and Thai numerals ๐–๙) alongside Basic Latin, Latin-1 Supplement, and common European diacritics.

To contribute to the project, visit github.com/ekkamai-foundry/ekkamai-vibe.
