Patiwat is a Thai–Latin sans-serif variable typeface family designed by Worachet Deeyai.

The family ships with a weight axis (wght 100–900) and full Thai character coverage (consonants, tone marks, vowels, Thai numerals ๐–๙) alongside Basic Latin, Latin-1 Supplement, and common European diacritics.

To contribute to the project, visit https://github.com/worachetdee/patiwat.
